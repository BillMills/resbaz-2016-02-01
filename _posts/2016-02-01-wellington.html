The Research Bazaar is a (free-of-charge) 3-day intensive conference where researchers come together to up-skill in ‘next generation digital research tools and skills’. In the spirit of a marketplace or bazaar, ResBaz is a highly participatory event where researchers from many different disciplines can learn, share knowledge and skills, and have fun!

Who should attend? Researchers at ALL levels from ALL disciplines. PhD and research Masters students are especially encouraged to apply. Research technologists who support researchers to do their job should also come along!

NOTE! The Wellington event is being held 26-28th April, not 1-3 Feb as elsewhere in the world. We are still working on the programme to bring you a broad range of digital research skills topics but you should register now to avoid missing out. Below are just some of the sessions that will be on offer.
