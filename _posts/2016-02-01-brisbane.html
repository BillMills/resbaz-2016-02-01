Below you'll find the training and events on offer in Brisbane - the location is QUT's Gardens Point campus. You can sign up here.

As a warmup for Brisbane ResBaz, we are running Software Carpentry instructor training on 18-19 January at The University of Queensland. Those new instructors will then teach sessions at ResBaz.

Are you interested in helping out, perhaps by running a session, giving a talk or just by volunteering some time? Fill out this short form to tell us what you're interested in.
